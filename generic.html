Career Update

Nihal was born on 22nd August ,2002 in Bihar. He then completed his schooling from "The Assembly Of God Church School, Tollygunj, Kolkata ". During his school days, he was very fond of playing cricket and volleyball. He completed his Class 10th in the year 2018 from ICSE board and secured 82.2%. He was awarded with a One Pointer (90+ in any subject) Certificate for scoring 90+ in Mathematics. Moving ahead he completed his Class 12th in PCM (With Computer Applications) from ISC board in the year 2020 and secured 84%. Again in the year 2020, he was awarded with a One Pointer Certificate for scoring 90+ in Computer Applications. Nihal then continued his studies toward Engineering. He appeared for JEE Mains in the year 2020 , and secured 79 percentile.

In the year 2020, Covid changed the complete way and standards of education . Nihal appeared for the SAAT 2020 exam which is an entrance exam for SOA University, Bhubaneshwar. He choosed Electrical and Electronics as his stream of specialzation. Currently in the final Semester of his Under-Grad course , he has an excellent CGPA of 8.75. Nihal was inclined toward computer and did many online courses during his entire B.tech career to develop his coding skills. Starting from JAVA, C++, Python , HTML .. till Web Dev. Nihal secured 5 Star gold badge on HackerRank for his coding skills in JAVA , he aims to solve the real life problems which can be simplified and mae easier with the help of computers and coding.
